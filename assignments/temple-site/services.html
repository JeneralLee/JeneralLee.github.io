Services & Amenities

Special Services

  • Free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for temple patrons who have traveled far and need to change and from church clothes.
  • A play room and low-cost, short-term babysitting services for children of patrons.
  • Long-term accommodations with kitchenette suites for full-time temple missionaries.
  • Inviting reception and sitting areas with temple history theme.
  • Business center that supports family search and ordinance work.

Amenities

  • Breakfast included
  • Exercise room
  • Indoor pool
  • Wi-fi, high-speed internet, Smart TVs
  • Onsite parking
  • Family history consultation available
  • Family ordinance card services

Reception Hall Services

  • Beautiful, traditional ballroom that can accomodate up to 150 guests
  • Optional hardwood dance floor
  • Table linens and chairs included with a lovely selection of ribbon accents in several color ways
  • Full menu catering including choice of hors d'oevres, starters, main dishes, sides bread, dessert, and beverages
  • Full kitchen available for preparing and serving your own menu
  • Special pricing from affiliated vendors for wedding cakes, invitations, and DJ services.
